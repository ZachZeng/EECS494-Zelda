Zelda Re-implement



Our custom level has changed the light in the game, which contains one dark room, one secret room and one torch room. Player need to use our new tool -- flashlight to go through these levels.

Our custom mechanic is based on the new tool -- flashlight. Player can use flashlight to detect secret doors, ghosts(new enemy) and lighten their path.
